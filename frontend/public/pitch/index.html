The Problem

Service businesses run on chaos.

4M+ salons, barbershops, tutors and photographers across Central Asia manage every appointment by hand. There is no localized tool built for them.

Bookings lost in chat

Appointments live in WhatsApp threads and phone calls — easily forgotten, double-booked, or never confirmed.

No digital presence

Customers can't find or book online. The business is invisible outside word-of-mouth.

Costly no-shows

No automated reminders means empty chairs, wasted hours and lost daily revenue.

No localized tool

Calendly and Booksy don't support Uzbek or the local market. Owners are left with nothing that fits.

Our Solution

One platform. Booking handled end-to-end.

Reserva gives every business a bookable profile and automates the entire appointment lifecycle.

24/7 Self-Booking

Customers book any time without a call — real-time availability, instant confirmation.

Business Profiles

A shareable page with services, prices, hours, gallery and reviews.

Auto Reminders

Telegram bot notifications confirm bookings and cut no-shows automatically.

Uzbek · Russian · EN

Fully trilingual UX built for how the region actually speaks and works.

Security Built-In

JWT + OTP + RBAC + reCAPTCHA protect every account and transaction.

Analytics

Owners see bookings, trends and customer activity at a glance.
